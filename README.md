# Retail Customer Retention Analytics (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail customer data for TARGET to understand
customer retention, churn patterns, channel performance, and customer lifetime value (CLV).

The analysis helps identify high-risk churn customers and provides actionable
business insights to improve customer loyalty.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Identify customers at high risk of churn
- Analyze customer segments contributing most to revenue
- Evaluate performance of sales and marketing channels
- Support data-driven retention and loyalty strategies

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- DAX (Measures & Calculated Columns)

## 📈 Key Insights
- A small percentage of customers contribute majority of total revenue (Pareto Principle)
- High-value repeat customers show higher purchase frequency and AOV
- Discount-heavy channels drive short-term sales but lower long-term retention
- Certain channels generate traffic but show low conversion and loyalty
